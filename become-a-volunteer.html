ABOUT "SPREADING SMILE FOUNDATION" :
This is a charity-based Registered Non Profit Organization under society registration act 1860 XX , Federal Board of Revenue and Punjab Charity Commission . The foundation was started by "Mr.Haseeb Ur Rehman" in 2016. This Foundation mainly working for two aspects i.e "Helping Needy and Community Development". To help needy this Foundation Targets needy people who are not begging but working hard for their family and self-respect. Over the last couple of years, the gathered amount has been donated to Watchmen, street vendors, and fruit walls ( who were in debt or were unable to pay their utility bills or need some medical or educational assistance, etc.) as a charity. To gather the help and Money, members of this Foundation go to different institutions and people and collect the donations. After collecting a suitable amount, members start going to Targeted person for distribution. And for community development we organize different volunteer programs where we give different exposures to our youth ,also we conduct different awareness program on different hot topics.
