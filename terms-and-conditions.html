Terms & Conditions

1. Attendance Requirement

Volunteers are expected to attend meetings, visits, training sessions, and assigned activities regularly. In case of absence, volunteers must inform the relevant team lead in advance with a valid reason.

2. Task Completion Policy

Points and evaluations will only be awarded for tasks completed properly and submitted within the assigned deadline.

3. Certificate Eligibility

Certificates will only be awarded to volunteers who:

  • Complete all assigned tasks within the given deadline.
  • Maintain 100% attendance.
  • Achieve the minimum point criteria set by the organization.
  • Follow the organization’s code of conduct throughout the program.

Participation in the program does not automatically guarantee a certificate.

4. Best Volunteer Selection

The “Best Volunteer” award will be based on overall performance, including:

  • Attendance
  • Task completion
  • Discipline
  • Teamwork
  • Communication
  • Contribution to activities

The decision of the organizing team will be final.

5. Late Submissions

Late submissions may receive reduced points or may not be accepted, depending on the delay and circumstances.

6. Code of Conduct

Volunteers are expected to maintain respectful and professional behavior with:

  • Fellow volunteers
  • Team leads
  • Children
  • Elderly individuals
  • Staff members of partner organizations
  • Beneficiaries and members of the public

Any inappropriate behavior, misconduct, harassment, discrimination, abusive language, or violation of organizational values may result in warning, suspension, or removal from the volunteer program.

7. Use of Photos & Videos

SSF reserves the right to use photos and videos taken during volunteer activities for social media, promotional, awareness, reporting, and documentation purposes.

8. Communication Responsibility

Volunteers are responsible for regularly checking official WhatsApp groups and communication channels for updates, announcements, deadlines, and instructions.

9. Fair Evaluation

All points, evaluations, certificates, leadership opportunities, and awards will be monitored and decided by the organizing team. The organization’s decisions will be considered final.

10. Respect for NGO Rules & Values

Volunteers agree to follow the mission, policies, ethical standards, and rules of SSF throughout the program duration.

11. Volunteer Membership & Removal

SSF reserves the right to suspend or terminate any volunteer’s participation at any time in cases of misconduct, policy violation, indiscipline, inactivity, or behavior harmful to the organization’s reputation or environment.

12. False Allegations & Harmful Conduct

Any hate speech, intentional misinformation, defamation, false accusations, or harmful actions against the organization, its members, or beneficiaries may result in disciplinary or legal action where applicable.

13. Agreement Confirmation

By submitting this form, I confirm that:

  • I have read and understood all the terms and conditions of the SSF Volunteer Program.
  • I agree to follow all organizational rules and guidelines.
  • I understand that participation in the program does not guarantee certificates, leadership positions, or awards.
  • I accept that the organization may take disciplinary action, including removal from the program, in case of misconduct or violation of these terms.
