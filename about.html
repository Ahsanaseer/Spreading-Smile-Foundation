ABOUT US

Spreading Smile Foundation is a charity-based organization that has been working since 2016 for the betterment of the less fortunate in our society. Founded by Mr. Haseeb ur Rehman, we support those in need financially, educationally, and through skill development.

We focus on helping hardworking individuals who silently struggle including watchmen, street vendors, students, and those in debt. We also support deserving students who cannot afford school or university fees, and have assisted patients battling cancer, diabetes, and lung diseases.

We have conducted free medical and eye camps in underprivileged areas to improve access to basic healthcare. Our team raises awareness and collects donations mostly through social media. We run volunteer programs, provide certificates, and select passionate youth to join our team. We also organize tree plantation drives and advocate for climate action.

Latest update : We are actively working to launch a blood bank to urgently provide blood to the patients

The world faces many challenges and instead of staying silent, it's time to stand up and become the change we want to see. Our mission can only be possible with your help and donations so let's spread smile , kindness and joy .
