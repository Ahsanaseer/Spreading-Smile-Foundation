About This Event

Our visit to "Bait ul Zaeef Oldage Home" where we spent time with them and listened to their hearts.

We played games with them and they enjoyed it so well. We arranged an iftar party, dinner, and distributed gifts and Eid clothes to them.

We tried to fill colors in their Eid. This all happened because of your donations.

Your donations brought smiles to many sad and silent faces. We are very thankful to everyone who contributed to this purpose.
