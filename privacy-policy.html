Privacy Policy

Spreading Smile Foundation App

1. Introduction

Spreading Smile Foundation ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you voluntarily provide details such as your name, contact number, email address, or location while signing up, making donations, or contacting us.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-related data such as your IP address, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used, pages visited, and time spent on the App.
  • Location Data (if applicable): With your permission, we may collect your location data to suggest nearby donation centers or aid locations.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Facilitate donations and aid distribution.
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Communicate with users regarding updates, campaigns, and donation opportunities.
  • Ensure compliance with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. However, we may share information:

  • With trusted third-party service providers for processing payments, analytics, or cloud storage.
  • When required by law enforcement agencies or government authorities.
  • With partner organizations working towards the same humanitarian goals, only with user consent.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Our App may include links to third-party websites or services (e.g., donation gateways, Google Maps). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them.

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete their personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data collection (which may affect App functionality).
  • Request a copy of their stored data.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted within the App, and users will be notified if required.
