With every passing day, I have observed the underprivileged community being neglected. I used to think," A positive change begins with a firm decision, either to do something for people or nothing at all," and finally I decided to go with the former.
I started this NGO because I couldn’t ignore the pain of hunger and neglect. I wanted to create hope, provide essentials, and ensure no one feels forgotten. Every meal we serve is a step toward dignity. Every smile we restore is a reminder that humanity still thrives.
Spreading Smile Foundation aims to ensure no one sleeps hungry by providing food and basic necessities, inspired by the desire to help needy people encountered on the streets. We believe that kindness can transform lives and restore hope and belief in humanity.
