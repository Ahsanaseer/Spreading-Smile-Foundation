Seeing the neglect of the underprivileged, I realized that positive change starts with a firm decision — I chose to take action and help those in need.
I started this NGO because I couldn’t ignore the pain of hunger and neglect. I wanted to create hope, provide essentials, and ensure no one feels forgotten.
Our NGO aims to ensure no one sleeps hungry by providing food and basic necessities, inspired by the desire to help needy people encountered on the streets.
